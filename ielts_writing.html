Introduction 句型
背景句（改写题目）
In recent years, there has been growing debate over whether [topic] is truly effective.
例：In recent years, there has been growing debate over whether increasing sports facilities truly improves public health.
两方观点句
While some argue that [view A], others contend that [view B].
例：While some argue that more gyms would encourage exercise, others contend that education and policy changes are far more effective.
立场句（Thesis Statement）
In my view, although [concession], [your position] is ultimately more [adjective].
例：In my view, although sports facilities play a role, raising public health awareness is ultimately more impactful.
Body 段落句型
Topic Sentence
One of the most compelling arguments in favour of [position] is that [point].
例：One of the most compelling arguments in favour of increased sports facilities is that they provide accessible venues for regular exercise.
Explanation 句
This is primarily because [reason], which in turn [consequence].
例：This is primarily because convenient access removes a key barrier to exercise, which in turn encourages more consistent physical activity.
Example 句
For instance, in countries such as [country], research has shown that [finding].
例：For instance, in countries such as Finland, research has shown that neighbourhoods with more public sports facilities report lower rates of obesity.
让步句
Admittedly, [opposing view]; however, this does not necessarily mean that [your counter].
例：Admittedly, building facilities requires considerable investment; however, this does not necessarily mean that the long-term health benefits are not worth the cost.
Conclusion 句型
重申立场
In conclusion, while both sides of the argument have merit, I would maintain that [position].
例：In conclusion, while both approaches have value, I would maintain that public awareness campaigns should take precedence over facility construction.
综合总结
On balance, the evidence suggests that [position], provided that [condition].
例：On balance, the evidence suggests that a multi-pronged approach is most effective, provided that governments prioritise long-term behavioural change.
高分复杂句型
倒装句（Only when）
Only when [condition] will [subject] [verb].
例：Only when governments invest in public education will societies see lasting improvements in health outcomes.
强调句（It is … that）
It is [emphasis], rather than [contrast], that [verb phrase].
例：It is a lack of awareness, rather than a lack of facilities, that lies at the heart of the public health crisis.
条件句（虚拟语气）
If governments were to [action], the long-term benefits would far outweigh the initial costs.
例：If governments were to invest more heavily in health education, the long-term benefits would far outweigh the initial costs.
Not only … but also
Not only does [subject + verb], but it also [second benefit].
例：Not only does regular exercise improve physical health, but it also has a well-documented positive effect on mental well-being.

用四段式框架写一篇完整Task 2，重点是结构正确，哪怕词汇简单也无妨。写完对照框架自查每段是否有topic sentence、解释、例子。

本阶段对应知识库
📖 词汇替换表 — 基础词升级
✍️ Introduction和Body句型
⚙️ 主谓一致 + 倒装句结构
第一周目标：能写出结构完整的Task 2（四段俱全），Introduction有清晰立场，每个Body段有PEE三步，语法主谓一致错误明显减少。
02
提速 — 词汇升级 + 句式多样
Day 8–14 · 第二周
词汇多样 复杂句型 限时写作
›
每天
限时Task 2（40分钟）
开始严格计时写作，不查字典，不修改超过5分钟。每次写完设定一个本篇专项目标：本篇不用任何基础词 / 本篇必须包含倒装句 / 本篇PEE每段三步完整。
每天
词汇升级训练
取上一篇essay，把所有基础词替换为高级词汇。目标：全文0个 important / good / bad / show / change 出现。同时检查衔接词是否多样（不能连续两段用同一个词）。
隔天
Task 1 图表描述练习
20分钟限时写一篇Task 1，重点练数据比较表达：a sharp rise / a gradual decline / peak at / account for 30%。必须包含Overview段落总结整体趋势。
本阶段对应知识库
📖 话题专项词汇 — 健康/教育/科技
✍️ 高分复杂句型 + 衔接词分类
🔗 介词搭配全表
第二周目标：写作词汇多样性提升（essay中基础词占比下降），至少有一个倒装句或强调句，Task 1能在20分钟内写完150词并包含Overview。
03
强化 — 准确率 + 语法精修
Day 15–18 · 第三周前段
语法精准 自评能力 Task1+2连写
›
每天
Task 1 + Task 2 连续写作（60分钟）
模拟真实考试节奏：先写Task 1（20分钟），再写Task 2（40分钟）。重点训练在连续写作状态下保持质量，避免Task 2因疲劳而结构散乱。
每天
四维度自评（TR/CC/LR/GRA）
写完后按官方四维度逐项评分，每项找出1个具体改进点（不能只写"需改进"，要写"第X句哪里改成什么"）。建立错题本记录高频错误。
每天
语法错误集中修复
整理错题本中最频繁出现的语法错误，针对每种错误写5个正确例句，直到不再犯同类错误为止。
本阶段对应知识库
⚙️ 语法要点 — 时态、拼写全表
✍️ Conclusion句型 + 让步句
🔗 复习全部介词搭配
本阶段目标：每篇Task 2语法错误少于4处，Task 1能准确描述数据趋势并有清晰Overview，四维度自评能找到具体改进点而非泛泛而谈。
04
冲刺 — 模拟实战 + 内化框架
Day 19–20 · 考前两天
全真模拟 轻松复习 心态调整
›
Day 19
最后一套全真写作模拟
完整模拟Task 1+2，严格计时60分钟，考场条件（不查字典、不参考任何资料）。写完后轻度检查明显语法错误即可，不要大幅修改，建立信心。
Day 19
框架句型默写
把Introduction和Conclusion的框架句型默写一遍，确保考场上自动化输出。同时回顾本月积累的10个最高频词汇替换，确保能灵活调用。
Day 20
考试当天策略
Task 1用20分钟写完即转Task 2，不要超时。Task 2第一段先改写题目+表明立场，不要照抄原题任何词组。写完后留5分钟检查拼写和明显语法错误。
考前快速复习
✍️ Introduction + Conclusion 框架句
📖 词汇替换 — 快速过一遍
⚙️ 拼写错误表 — 最后确认
冲刺目标：框架已内化，不需要思考结构只需专注内容。词汇替换自动化，不会在考场上搜索词汇。相信自己20天的积累，放松发挥。
